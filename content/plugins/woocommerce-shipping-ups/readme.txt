=== WooCommerce UPS Shipping  ===

WooCommerce UPS Shipping allows a store to obtain shipping rates for your orders dynamically via the UPS Shipping API.


=== Installation ===

 * Purchase the extension and download the zip file from WooThemes.com
 * Login to your WordPress dashboard. Click on Plugins | Add New from the left hand menu
 * Click on the "Upload" option, then click "Browse" to select the zip file from your computer.
 * After the zip file has been selected press the "Install Now" button.
 * On the Plugins page, find the row for the "WooCommerce UPS Shipping" plugin and press "Activate"
 
 
=== Setup ===
 
 * In your WordPress admin area go to WooCommerce > Settings and click on the "Shipping" tab
 * In the Shipping Options row, click on the "UPS" link
 * You will need an account from UPS.com in order to complete the setup
 * To obtain an Access Key go to the following URL and follow the steps in the "How to Get Started" box:
 	https://www.ups.com/upsdeveloperkit
